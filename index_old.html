Learn More. Always.

Whether it be a new programming language or how to make an old table look new again, it is very important to always be learning.

If no one else can do it, Do it yourself.

I would add that even if it can be done by someone else, doing on your own is preferred. Although sometimes faster and easier, if the problem is not even attempted to be solved on your own, there will be no growth.

Be humble. Be resourceful.

By always helping others learn, weak spots can be found and skills can be practiced. Seeking help is better than just asking for the answer.
